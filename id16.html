"Walkin' in a Wiener Wonderland" (click here for more info and map)

Dapper Dachshunds dressed in Holiday Attire!
Enjoy an afternoon walk in the park with your doxie!
Christmas Concert and Sing-Along
2:00 at the Organ Pavilion
Plan a picnic anywhere you want.
Don't forget your camera!

This is not a centralized picnic at one specific location!
(If you want to have a picnic somewhere in the park,
that is up to you.)

The idea is to get your own Dachshunds out in the park for a walk 
"Dressed in Holiday Attire",
enjoy the day, and walk along the Prado in Balboa Park down to hear the Christmas Organ Concert.
(Sit in the back area along the steps.)
