THE HALLO-WIENER PICNIC

The San Diego Dachshund Club’s Hallo-Wiener Picnic, held in Balboa Park the Sunday before Halloween each year, is an event you don’t want to miss. Bring your camera to record all the Dachshunds that are dressed as "dog-o-lanterns", "dog-goblins" and every other costumed beastie you can imagine. The Dachshunds enjoy showing off their finery and owners enjoy the camaraderie!

For those eager to get down to Balboa Park and walk around "before" the picnic and show off their dogs in costume, at say 11 am. Meet at the Rueben H. Fleet Science Center at the Fountain, then at about 12 noon leisurely walk down to the picnic grounds behind the Aerospace Museum on the south side for the Hallowiener Picnic put on by The San Diego Dachshund Club. Be advised this may be a bit of a walk for our four legged ones so take it real easy and bring lots of water. There is a public water fountain at the corner of the Prado on the way.

After the picnic if you wish one more chance to show off your creativity by walking back up to the Organ Pavilion anytime after 2pm concert begins because we have special permission to be called up by the Organist at roughly 2:45 to strut our stuff across the stage in front of the organ. Thereby giving all the concert goers a view of and to take pictures of our Most Valuable Possessions, our costumed friends, pals, fur babies and four legged children. Dress in costume along with them if you wish and don't forget the water. All dogs on leash at all times and owner must be responsible for their behavior and clean up. The City of San Diego, Balboa Park, San Diego Organ Society and The San Diego Dachshund Club are not to be held liable.

"The Phantom of the Dachshunds" was performed
by Carol Williams, San Diego Civic Organist,
at the Spreckels Organ Pavilion 
Balboa Park, San Diego,
Sunday, October 30th, 2005
at approximately 2:50 p.m. near the end of her
Halloween Concert.
 
Dachshunds in costume (optional), and their owners,
were invited
to rendezvous at the right rear seating area of the Organ Pavilion
prior to that time and then parade about the Organ Pavilion as
Carol introduced and then played "The Phantom of the Dachshunds"
for the San Diego Dachshund Club.
