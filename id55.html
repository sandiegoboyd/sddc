San Diego Dachshund Club Application

Individual membership $20.00 Family membership $25.00
Since SDDC has fully gone to Emailing of Newsletters, make
sure the Newsletter Editor has a current address for you.
Fully paid membership is due on July 1st of each year and
includes the monthly newsletter via Email.

Full or Associate membership begins after dues are paid, two meetings are attended(full) and a 3/4 majority vote of approval by the membership. Full or Associate, the difference is only in attendance and voting privileges.

ADVANTAGES OF JOINING;
1. Dachshund related events
2. AKC regulations
3. Dog Health Information
4. Obedience and Show Training
5. Annual Dachshund Shows
6. Dachshund Picnics

Club membership is not determined by pedigree, ownership, location, profession or affiliation and is granted without regard to nationality, race, religion, sex, or disability. Only the love of animals, show dogs and pets, especially Dachshunds!

(A "MEMBERSHIP FORM" is available at top of this page for downloading and printing out.)

Upon joining you will be acquainted with breeders and owners of miniature and standards. Plus the varieties; smooth, longhair and wirehair plus all the colors possible with selective breeding; red, brown, black, tan, white, dapple (a combination of colors), grey and blue. Blue is a rare light color similar to grey, a very selective and unique coloration.

The Club offers two membership options at the same cost, Full and Associate. With limited voting privileges, Associate is intended for both out-of-state members and local members who choose not to participate in the monthly meetings. With attendance at two meetings associate converts to Full membership which offers voting rights and hopefully active participation in the meetings and club activities.
