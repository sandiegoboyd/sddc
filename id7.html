Dachshund Meetup, try this if not on Facebook
Looking for a monthly meetup for you and your dachshund? Several SDDC members have asked this easy venue be listed for you to sign-up and receive email notices. Basically it's a site which organizes monthly casual meetups for people who are fond of dachshunds. It creates more opportunities for socializing one's dachshund and meeting others who share the same interest in dachshunds. This is a non-SDDC event offered for those seeking more.
