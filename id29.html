The Vernal Equidox 2007- Balboa Park, San Diego
Click photo to enlarge the image

Everyone met in Balboa Park under the trees up by the main fountain near the Science Center.
Later they paraded down the length of the Prado toward the Spreckels Organ Pavilon.
The tourists were quite curious as to why all the Doxies were there.
...of course we explained it was Spring and
"The Vernal EquiDox"...When the Doxies Return to Balboa Park!

The Vernal Equidox 2007

Carol Williams, San Diego Civic Organist, generously accomodated the Dachshund Club by playing a couple of melodies for us. Carol announced the Vernal EquiDox event, and invited the Doxies and their owners to parade across the beautiful Spreckels Organ Pavilion stage, for their fifteen seconds of fame, as she played!
