Hi there!
Just wanted to let you all know about some upcoming events at the San Diego Humane Society and SPCA. If you'd like more info on any of them, please let me know. Thanks for all you do to help animals!

Stacey

 

Pet Loss Support Group

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA

Wednesday, September 14th at 6:30 p.m. at 5500 Gaines Street, San Diego 92110

Thursday, September 22nd at 6:30 p.m. at 572 Airport Road, Oceanside 92058

Saturday, September 24th at 10 a.m. at 5500 Gaines Street, San Diego 92110

The Pet Loss Support Group is open to those ages 10 and up, including pet parents who are considering or preparing for euthanasia. Sessions are free; however, donations to support the program are greatly appreciated. Please call (619) 299-7012, extension 2311, to make a reservation or for more information. www.sdhumane.org.

Doggie Café

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA

Friday, September 9th from 6-8 p.m. at 572 Airport Road, Oceanside 92058
Friday, September 30th from 6-8 p.m. at 5500 Gaines Street, San Diego 92110
Bring your friendly, on-leash dog to socialize with other dogs at this fun socialization event! Dogs should be friendly and on-leash. One dog per human handler please. Dogs should be at least 4 months old and up-to-date on all vaccines, including rabies, to attend. Complimentary desserts and dog treats provided. Behavior and Training specialists "on-paw" to answer training questions. $10 suggested donation for the event. 619-299-7012 ext 2230. www.sdhumane.org.

Home-school Lessons at the San Diego Humane Society

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA

Friday, September 9th from 10-11:30 at 572 Airport Road, Oceanside 92058

Friday, September 9th from 1:30-3 at 5500 Gaines Street, San Diego 92110

Children ages seven-12 are invited to learn about animals at these programs just for home-schoolers. $7 per child, per session. Reservations required. Please call 619-299-7012 ext. 2320 for a reservation. www.sdhumane.org.

First Aid for Dogs and Cats

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA

Sunday, September 11th from 1 p.m.-5 p.m. at 572 Airport Road, Oceanside 92058

This American Red Cross class covers treatments for various pet emergencies, including choking, shock, poisoning, and broken bones. $60 per person with choice of dog or cat book/DVD set. ($80 for both sets). Reservations required. Please call 619-243-3424 or check www.sdhumane.org for more information.

Tots and Tales

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA
Tuesday, September 13th at 10:30 at 5500 Gaines Street, SD 92110

Wednesday, September 21st at 10:30 a.m. at 572 Airport Road, Oceanside 92058

Bring your preschooler for an interactive story-time complete with animal stories, crafts, and of course, ANIMALS at the San Diego Humane Society and SPCA! Playgroups please call to arrange a private program. $3 per child. Reservations required. 619-243-3432. www.sdhumane.org.

 

Alternative Therapies for Animals lecture

San Diego Humane Society and SPCA
Thursday, September 15th from 6:30 p.m.- 8 p.m. at 5500 Gaines Street, San Diego 92110

Please join us to learn about various holistic options that may be used in conjunction with traditional veterinary care to keep your dog healthier and happier by keeping a healthful balance to optimize patient care through: Canine Massage, TuiNa, Cold LASER, Reiki, Emotional Freedom Technique and Muscle Testing. Cost is $12 per person and includes materials, light snacks and non-alcoholic refreshments. Please note classes are for people only - please leave your great pets at home. Register on-line or call (619) 243-3424 for more information. www.sdhumane.org.
